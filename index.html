OUR EVENTS

BlogCreate2.0

Blogcreate was a blogging and web development event, with 60 participants competing with each other to create a functional webpage with a creatively written article in it. Speakers, including Mr. Sarath Babu, founder of Sarath Babu’s Lifestyle Blogs, Mr. Diwakar Badiger, co-founder of Careerboat and Mr. Tathagat Thapliyal, a front-end developer from Cred, gave valuable insights to students on a wide array of topics, including the art of writing, UI/UX and other aspects of web development.
The participants, in teams of two, developed visually appealing and responsive web pags, while writing articles on a topic given to them, also improving on their research skills as they explored more information into their topics and web development. They were then judged on the basis of the originality and creativity of their writeups, and their aesthetic appeal and responsiveness of their webpages.

CodeQuest

Codequest was a fast-paced fun event into the joys of coding. Students were given a a set of codes in a language of their preference, including Java, Python and C++, and had to predict the output of the code. The students who could correctly predict the output in the shortest amount of time achieved the most score. Participants had a fun and exciting time during the event, and freshers had a first glimpse into the world of programming, providing them with a valuable first experience in university.
