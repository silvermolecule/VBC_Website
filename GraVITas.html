Our Events

BlogCreate2.0

The event will be conducted in a span of two days for a total of 9 hours, 4 hours on the first day and 5 hours on the second day.
On the first day, we will have our guest speakers talking about blogging and teaching website development. On the second day, the participants will be given their themes and have to create a blog and a website based on that theme.

CodeQuest

This will be a fun event where the participants will be given five different programming codes and they will have to guess the question given corresponding to the codes they are provided in the problem statement. Programming languages used will be Python, C++, Java.
